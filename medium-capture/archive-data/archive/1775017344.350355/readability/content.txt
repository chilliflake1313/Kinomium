It is not something you can hold or hear. It exists only as an exact mathematical construction, defined by a simple equation. And yet, despite its simplicity, it behaves in a way that feels almost impossible.Imagine this horn stretching out into space, longer and longer, becoming thinner and thinner, never quite ending.Now consider this.
